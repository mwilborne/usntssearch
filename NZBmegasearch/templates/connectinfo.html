Connection to SICKBEARD
Sickbeard connectivity is always active.
In Sickbeard: go to Config/Search Providers and add a custom Newznab provider, no apikey needed.

Connection to COUCHPOTATO
Couchpotato connectivity is always active.
In Couchpotato: go to Settings/Providers and add a custom Newznab provider, no apikey needed.

CHROME extensions
jparkerweb developed two Chrome extensions that natively support mega.nzbx.co
Chrome SickBeard Xtender: adds links to scene search sites for all of your episodes. Helpful for when SickBeard can't find it on its own.
Chrome NZBDrone Xtender: adds links to scene search sites for all of your episodes. Helpful for when NZBDrone can't find it on its own.
